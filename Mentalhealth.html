Mental Health and Wellbeing

This website will give an overview on how to manage and improve your mental health and wellbeing.

Types of mental health issues:

  1. Stress
    • A common problem that a high percentage of people face.
    • Result of feeling under pressure or threatened.
    • Often occurs when we feel like things are out of our control.
  2. Depression
    • A condition that may affect your life for a small or large amount of time.
    • Can lead to low self esteem and finding it hard to feel happiness.
    • Medication can help.
  3. Anxiety
    • A feeling that everything may go wrong at any point, often for no particular reason
    • This may tie into stress and/or depression
    • Meditation may help.

Methods of bettering your headspace:

  • Go for a walk
  • Remove yourself from stressful situations if you can't hack it
  • Have a bath
  • Talk about your problems with a friend
  • Do some push-ups

Physical Health:

Physical health and mental health are closely related. A healthy lifestyle helps your body to produce and regulate the correct levels of hormones. This may not always be the case for all people, such as those genetically predisposed to mental illness, however for the majority of the population 30 minutes of exercise a day will not only make you feel better physically, but also mentally.
